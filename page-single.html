With 12 Days on the clock, you can't afford to miss a beat. Or miss the European Highlights. The perfect loop from Paris, it will see you partying in Amsterdam one moment and driving through scenic of Swiss the next. With the perfect combination different Europe destination you will be driven from Eiffel tower to Atonium, you will be amazed with the history of Germany on the way to Swiss Alps. Finally the beauty of Mt. Titlis will amaze you.
Finally you will end your tour with good memories.
Itinerary
  • Day 1 – Paris (France)

    Arrive at Airport & transfer to the hotel. The rest of the day is at leisure so that you can relax after your long-haul flight.

    Please note: The Tour Manager will meet you once you reach the hotel. The standard check-in time is 1500 hrs.
